Overview

As a Software Engineer and Design Intern at Skywalk, a startup specializing in silent speech technology, I played a key role in designing and developing their iOS application, seamlessly integrating this innovative technology with proprietary wireless earbuds. My responsibilities included crafting intuitive user experiences, creating high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, and collaborating with HCI designers to ensure the app addressed the needs of diverse user groups, particularly individuals with disabilities.
