planet.

cs 147 final project

project details:

Planet is a mental health-focused app designed and developed as part of the CS 147: Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction Design course at Stanford University. Over 10 weeks, my team and I went through the full user-centered design cycle, including research, ideation, prototyping, and evaluation.

timeline:

september 2024 - december 2024

what i did:

web development, ios development, user needfinding research, ui/ux design

website:

Along with our final app, we were required to create a website for our deliverables. I helped design and develop the Planet website, taking care to incorporate our design values. This site won the "Best Website" award at the CS 147 Final Expo. If you wish to see our design process in greater detail, please visit https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs147/projects/Technology-for-Mental-Health/Planet/
